FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Pips Forex Academy?

Pips Forex Academy is an online educational platform that provides comprehensive training on forex trading. Our mission is to equip traders with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in the forex market.

Who can join Pips Forex Academy?

Anyone interested in learning about forex trading, from beginners to experienced traders, can join our academy. We offer courses tailored to different skill levels.

How do I sign up for a course?

You can sign up for a course by visiting our Courses page, selecting the course you're interested in, and following the registration instructions.

What is the duration of each course?

Course durations vary. Each course page provides detailed information on the time commitment required.

What topics are covered in the courses?

Our courses cover a wide range of topics, including forex basics, technical analysis, fundamental analysis, risk management, trading strategies, and more.

Are there any additional resources available?

Yes, we provide a range of additional resources, including ebooks, webinars, and a community forum where you can interact with fellow traders.

Do you provide any support for students?

Yes, we offer 24/7 support for our students. You can contact our support team via email or live chat for any assistance you need.

How much do the courses cost?

Course prices vary. Please visit the Pricing Section for detailed information on course fees.
